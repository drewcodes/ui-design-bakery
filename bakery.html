The Sweet Bites Story

Sweet Bites was started by Sarah, a stay-at-home mom, who has an endless love for baking. Having graduated from a pastry program at one of the most prestigious culinary schools, Sarah has created countless cakes, pastries and other fine desserts. All items on our menu are handcrafted daily and delivered straight to your door. We hope you enjoy what Sweet Bites has to offer.
